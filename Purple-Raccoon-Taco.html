PURPLE RACCOON LORE:

The purple raccoon god Azeban Ruled over the lands of Timmeh, killing anyone who dared to not give him cheese. Overtime Azeban became morbidly obese, overtime his condition worsened and he floated around a laughing stock. So he confided in his friend, the cheese god, whom gave him The Stick of Purple Power. Using The Stick of Purple Power, Azeban cursed everyone to be extremely overweight...
